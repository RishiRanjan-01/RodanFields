MEET OUR FOUNDERS

Dr. Katie Rodan and Dr. Kathy Fields founded Rodan + Fields on the belief that everyone deserves great skin. They’re dermatologists,
innovators and entrepreneurs who create revolutionary products that deliver real results.

STANFORD-TRAINED DERMATOLOGISTS

Their ongoing experience as dermatologists and product
formulators uniquely positions them to create innovative products
that address skin concerns effectively.

ENTREPRENEURS

Their spirit for sharing knowledge led them to use a Consumer
Connected Commerce model that allows Consultants to impart
their experience directly to Customers, thereby becoming
entrepreneurs as well.

LIFE-CHANGING SKINCARE

Their shared passion for healthy-looking skin led them to develop
their proprietary Multi-Med Therapy system that takes the
guesswork out of skincare and delivers visible results.

GIVING BACK

Their commitment to making the world better inspired them to
found the Rodan + Fields Prescription for Change Foundation,
which gives young people access to life-changing empowerment
programs.

THE EVOLUTION OF R+F

After meeting at Stanford University School of Medicine in the 1980s, Dr. Katie Rodan and Dr. Kathy Fields develop Proactiv® Solution.

The Doctors launch Rodan + Fields with Regimens for a range of skin concerns like signs of aging. Rodan + Fields is acquired by The Estée Lauder Companies, Inc. in 2003.

The Doctors announce a company buy-back from The Estée Lauder Companies Inc. Relaunching in 2008, they empower others to become entrepreneurs as R+F Independent Consultants.

Products including REVERSE Lightening, REDEFINE ACUTE CARE and the AMP MD System are recognized for innovation and efficacy with awards from Allure, Forbes and TotalBeauty.

The Doctors continue to build the company. Rodan + Fields becomes the #1 Skincare Brand in the U.S. in 20161 and is the #1 Fastest Growing Skincare Brand in the U.S. over the last 6 years2.

Celebrating 10 years of Life-changing Skincare, Rodan + Fields is named the #1 Skincare Brand in North America3 and is the fastest growing beauty and personal care brand in the U.S. in 20174.
